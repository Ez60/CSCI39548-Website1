Elephant

img source https://files.worldwildlife.org/wwfcmsprod/images/African_Elephant_Kenya_112367/story_full_width/qxyqxqjtu_WW187785.jpg

The elephant is a large land animal. The elephant has a trunk that it uses for eating, drinking, grasping things and breathing. It also has tusks as weapons and as a tool to move objects.
